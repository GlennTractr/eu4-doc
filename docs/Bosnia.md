**Bosnia** is a landlocked country in [Eastern Europe](/Eastern_Europe "Eastern Europe"). It starts out surrounded by [![Flag of Hungary](/images/thumb/6/6a/Hungary.png/20px-Hungary.png)](/Hungary "Hungary") [Hungary](/Hungary "Hungary") and [![Flag of Serbia](/images/thumb/7/76/Serbia.png/20px-Serbia.png)](/Serbia "Serbia") [Serbia](/Serbia "Serbia"). It also borders [![Flag of Venice](/images/thumb/e/e1/Venice.png/20px-Venice.png)](/Venice "Venice") [Venice](/Venice "Venice") to the west , [![Flag of Herzegovina](/images/thumb/f/f8/Herzegovina.png/20px-Herzegovina.png)](/Herzegovina "Herzegovina") [Herzegovina](/Herzegovina "Herzegovina") to the south, and [![Flag of Croatia](/images/thumb/9/94/Croatia.png/20px-Croatia.png)](/Croatia "Croatia") [Croatia](/Croatia "Croatia") to the north.

![Strategy](/images/thumb/a/a2/Years_of_separatism.png/36px-Years_of_separatism.png "Strategy") _The below is **one of many** player suggested strategies for **Bosnia**. Bear in mind, due to the dynamic nature of the game, it may unfold differently for other players._

### Stepping into seven league boots\[[edit](/index.php?title=Bosnia&veaction=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Stepping into seven league boots") | [edit source](/index.php?title=Bosnia&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Stepping into seven league boots")\]

#### Overview of Bosnia\[[edit](/index.php?title=Bosnia&veaction=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Overview of Bosnia") | [edit source](/index.php?title=Bosnia&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Overview of Bosnia")\]

Ragusa would normally be the first thought for expansions, but it starts guaranteed by the [![Flag of Ottomans](/images/thumb/8/89/Ottomans.png/20px-Ottomans.png)](/Ottomans "Ottomans") [Ottomans](/Ottomans "Ottomans") Empire. It can help to have a claim on them, and in the event the Ottomans get into a very taxing war, they may not honour their guarantee. Serbia would be a logical second though for expansion. Assuming a threatened posture towards the Ottomans should help garner an [alliance](/Alliance "Alliance") with their [rivals](/Rivals "Rivals"). Such alliances will serve well to prevent Ottoman aggression. Most often, [![Flag of Poland](/images/thumb/9/99/Poland.png/20px-Poland.png)](/Poland "Poland") [Poland](/Poland "Poland"), [![Flag of Hungary](/images/thumb/6/6a/Hungary.png/20px-Hungary.png)](/Hungary "Hungary") [Hungary](/Hungary "Hungary") or [![Flag of Austria](/images/thumb/7/7f/Austria.png/20px-Austria.png)](/Austria "Austria") [Austria](/Austria "Austria") will have rivalled the Ottomans and would be suitable alliance partners. Do keep in mind that all of these are great powers, and sometimes they will be on a collision course, and Bosnia will have to play a balancing game of choosing between allies. It is worth considering that taking Dalmatia from Venice would allow, [Emperor](/Holy_Roman_Empire#Emperor "Holy Roman Empire") willing, to join the [HRE](/HRE "HRE"). This would protect the player from both the Ottomans as well as a potentially belligerent Hungary. If Austria starts rivalled to Venice, it is possible to call them into a war against Venice, promising land. Having joined the HRE it is much less challenging to expand therein, and middle and upper [Italy](/Italy "Italy") become much more viable targets.

If Austria is weakened, build relations with Poland and [![Flag of Lithuania](/images/thumb/d/d9/Lithuania.png/20px-Lithuania.png)](/Lithuania "Lithuania") [Lithuania](/Lithuania "Lithuania"), and if they are not rivalling [![Flag of Bohemia](/images/thumb/4/41/Bohemia.png/20px-Bohemia.png)](/Bohemia "Bohemia") [Bohemia](/Bohemia "Bohemia"), then build relations with them as well. Once Bosnia chooses an ally, enter their wars and support them. It is extremely important to raise the trust between Bosnia and the ally, since that will cause them to support Bosnia in wars regardless of their relations to.

#### The third way\[[edit](/index.php?title=Bosnia&veaction=edit&section=4 "Edit section: The third way") | [edit source](/index.php?title=Bosnia&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: The third way")\]

Rather than pushing into the HRE or antagonizing the Balkans, the player can instead conquer middle or lower Italy. They have rich provinces and high [manpower](/Manpower "Manpower"), and are excellent for [trade](/Trade "Trade"). This will cause a lot of [aggressive expansion](/Aggressive_expansion "Aggressive expansion") in the surrounding area, so going slowly or taking a break and conquering some Balkan land may be ideal.

#### The HRE game\[[edit](/index.php?title=Bosnia&veaction=edit&section=5 "Edit section: The HRE game") | [edit source](/index.php?title=Bosnia&action=edit&section=5 "Edit section: The HRE game")\]

Due to Bosnian national ideas and position, contending for Emperor by the Mid game is possible. Assuming that Emperorship is achieved it might be better to not fully unify the empire, but instead, pass all the reforms except the final one, leading to a massive influx of troops and gold trough vassals.

### General tips\[[edit](/index.php?title=Bosnia&veaction=edit&section=6 "Edit section: General tips") | [edit source](/index.php?title=Bosnia&action=edit&section=6 "Edit section: General tips")\]

The Venice trade node is much more valuable than the Ragusa trade node, expanding there can be a significant source of trade income. Watch the political landscape. If the Ottomans have just lost most of their manpower in a war against the [Mamluks](/Mamluks "Mamluks") or [Qara Qoyunlu](/Qara_Qoyunlu "Qara Qoyunlu"), it's could well be an excellent time to strike at them.

A useful navy is not possible unless the player has a naval forcelimit close to the Ottomans. Although the Ottomans usually field a large galley based navy, they often do not upgrade all of them to the most recent type. So it can be possible to beat their navy with a somewhat smaller one, as long it is more up to date.

### Choice of ideas\[[edit](/index.php?title=Bosnia&veaction=edit&section=7 "Edit section: Choice of ideas") | [edit source](/index.php?title=Bosnia&action=edit&section=7 "Edit section: Choice of ideas")\]

*   Offensive, defensive, quality, and quantity help field a more powerful army. Bosnian ideas and starting situation are well suited for Defensive-Religious / Influence-Quantity ideas, as each group unlocks +1 attrition Policies.
*   Economic will help Bosnia battle inflation if Kosovo is taken from Serbia, as well as helping with autonomy reduction.
*   Influence and diplomatic will help any bid to become the emperor of the HRE. Influence in particular synergizes with Bosnia's fifth national idea, which encourages having strong vassals.
*   Early Innovative ideas can lead to snowballing Innovativeness, reducing all power costs significantly. Innovative also lowers both advisor and Institution embracement costs.
